    Galaxy Collapse

    A Speedcore remix of 'Heian Alien' from Touhou 12 by French producer Kurokotei. This song is known in many rhythm game communities for its extreme BPM. Permission granted by Team Shanghai Alice"

    Night of Nights

    "Night of Nights" (Japanese: ナイト・オブ・ナイツ, Naito obu Naitsu), also known as "Night of Knights" or "Knight of Knights", is a musical arrangement based off the theme of character Sakuya Izayoi from the bullet hell vertical shooter game Touhou Project. This fast-paced song has since became one of the most popular Touhou remixes, being featured in numerous MAD videos and YouTube Poop Music Videos (YTPMVs), along with musical covers and parodies, and as well as being a contributing factor to the spread of popularity for the Touhou Project series.

    The Empress

    Track 9 from Japanese heavy metal doujin band UNDEAD CORPORATION's 2012 album "Tyrant". Arrangement of 'Embodiment of the Scarlet Devil' by ZUN. Permission granted from Team Shanghai Alice. The source theme is commonly known as Flandre's Theme, or the theme of Flandre Scarlet.

    CENSORED!!

    CENSORED!! is a remix of ハルトマンの妖怪少女, the extra boss theme of the Touhou Project game 東方地霊殿
